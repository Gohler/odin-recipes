Chocolate Avocado Smoothie

Description

This Chocolate Avocado Smoothie is creamy, perfectly sweet, and beautifully thick. Made with ripe avocados, a little cocoa powder, and chocolate milk, it ready within 5 minutes and makes for a wonderful healthy breakfast smoothie or snack.
