Mac and Cheese

Description

A seemingly simple combination of macaroni and melted cheeseâ€”is one of the most popular dishes in the United States. The preferred American combination consists of curved macaroni pasta and Cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups milk
  • 1 lb elbow macaroni(455 g), dry
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese(200 g)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil.
  2. Add the pasta and stir constantly until the pasta is cooked, about 10 minutes.
  3. Turn off the heat, then add the cheddar. Stir until the cheese is melted and the pasta is evenly coated.
  4. Enjoy!
